# Project-RepNexa
# 📊 Repnexa — Healthcare Field Analytics Platform

**Repnexa** is a data-driven field force management and analytics platform built for pharmaceutical and healthcare sales teams. It enables medical representatives, first-line managers, and head-office users to digitize daily call reporting, track field activity, and analyze territory performance in real time — eliminating manual Excel-based reporting and improving operational visibility across the organization.

🌐 **Product Website:** https://repnexa.com  

---

## 🚀 Core Capabilities

Repnexa provides a complete operational view of field activity across doctors, chemists, territories, and sales teams.

### 🩺 Doctor Visit Management
- Log and validate doctor visits  
- Identify missed doctors and coverage gaps  
- Track call frequency and visitation history  

### 💊 Chemist Coverage Analytics
- Monitor product availability by outlet  
- Analyze territory-wise chemist coverage  
- Identify distribution and promotion gaps  

### 💸 Expense & Mileage Tracking
- Daily expense and travel logging  
- Monthly summaries and reimbursement reports  
- Audit-ready data for finance teams  

### 📈 Territory Performance Analytics
- Geographic coverage trends  
- Rep-wise and territory-wise performance  
- Data-driven planning and optimization  

### ⚙️ Admin & Master Data Management
- Manage doctors, chemists, reps, and territories  
- Configure product lists and team structures  
- Control user access and permissions  

### 📱 Digital DCR (Daily Call Report)
- Fast, mobile-friendly daily reporting  
- Real-time submission and validation  
- Eliminates paper-based and WhatsApp reporting

## 🧠 Why Repnexa Exists

Healthcare field teams generate large volumes of operational data every day — but most organizations still rely on **manual reports, Excel files, and delayed summaries**.

Repnexa transforms this into a **live operational intelligence system** that allows managers to:
- See what is happening in the field today  
- Identify coverage and compliance issues instantly  
- Make better decisions using real data, not assumptions
